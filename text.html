He is a notorious outlaw who serves as the lead enforcer of the Van der Linde gang, as well as their deadliest member. He assists his mentor Dutch van der Linde in trying to live the dream of living outside of the law, but after some mishaps and tragedies, Arthur begins to have doubts about his loyalty to the gang's leader. Throughout the game, he attempts to either achieve his redemption for his crimes or become even more ruthless and cold-blooded in his life. He is also the best friend of John Marston and the arch-nemesis of Micah Bell, Andrew Milton and Colm O'Driscoll.
